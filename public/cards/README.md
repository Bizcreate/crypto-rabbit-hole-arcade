# Gen1 Card Images

Place your 75 Gen1 card images in this folder with the following naming convention:

- gen1-1.png
- gen1-2.png
- gen1-3.png
- ...
- gen1-75.png

Also include:
- CardBack-Final.png (the card back design)

The pack minter will automatically load these images when cards are revealed.
Cards will be displayed in the inventory as NFTs after being pulled from packs.
